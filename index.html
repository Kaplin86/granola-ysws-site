How does this work?


Create a breakout clone / brickbreaker in GODOT.
Publish your game to a hosting platform (like itch.io) for the world to see...
...and get free granola bars (and any other bar-shaped treats)!

Requirements

  • You are a high schooler (under 18)
  • Your game must be made in Godot.
  • Your game must be exported for web.
  • Your game must be published to a hosting platform (like itch.io).
  • Your game must be a brickbreaker / breakout clone.
  • Your game must be original, created for this YSWS specifically.
  • NO AI is allowed for code or imagery. Though, you can use AI to assist with the development process (like learning or coming up with ideas).
  • Your game must be open source.


Lets get some snacks!!!!


You can get multiple grant amounts based on the project.

Frequently Asked


  • Where do I ask for help?

    For general questions, ask in #granola in slack. For questions about granola, DM or ping @kaplin86 in Slack. For questions about club meetings, DM @jps in Slack.


  • What is the grant valid for?

    The grant is intended for any bar-shaped snacks / foods. I use granola as an example, most of the time it is found in bar shapes. Candy bars, snack bars, and protein bars would all count.


  • Can I use something other than godot?

    This program is meant to be a simple, beginner-friendly introduction to Godot (hence why its used in the guide), so no.
